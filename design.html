About Experience Design Projects

Design

This section highlights my mechanical and mechatronics design experience gained through professional and co-op roles. The focus is on design intent, constraints, and engineering decision-making rather than proprietary system details.

Table and Guarding

  • Designed machine tables and guarding systems using aluminum extrusions
  • Used Lexan panels to separate machine zones and protect components

Focus: Machine safety, guarding standards, system layout

Paper Reject Assembly

  • Designed high-speed paper reject system using an air knife
  • Created durable sheet metal components with weld nuts
  • Considered manufacturability and maintenance access

Focus: Sheet metal, pneumatics, DFM

Sensor Assembly

  • Designed adjustable sensor assemblies for inspecting pallet empty
  • Used round shafts and brackets for flexible positioning
  • Improved repeatability and ease of setup

Focus: Sensors, adjustability, fixture design

Hot Plate

  • Designed hot plate enclosure and supporting structure
  • Considered thermal isolation and operator safety
  • Ensured structural integrity under operating conditions

Focus: Thermal systems, safety, mechanical layout

Electrical Enclosures

  • Designed electrical enclosures with integrated HMI interfaces
  • Ensured accessibility for maintenance and serviceability

Focus: Enclosures, integration, serviceability

Heatsink Assembly Fixtures

  • Designed fixtures for heatsink and heat pipe assembly during epoxy curing
  • Selected aluminum and Delrin for precision and thermal considerations
  • Balanced accessibility with part alignment and repeatability

Focus: Precision fixtures, assembly support

Camera Mount

  • Designed rigid camera and lens mounting systems for vision applications
  • Ensured precise alignment and stability under machine vibration
  • Considered adjustability for calibration and maintenance

Focus: Vision systems, precision mounting, adjustability
